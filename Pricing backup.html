Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our SaaS pricing model.

Do I need my own server?

No. All SaaS plans are fully hosted on our secure cloud infrastructure. We handle servers, maintenance, backups, and scaling automatically.

Can I change plans later?

Yes! Upgrade or downgrade anytime based on your user count. Changes take effect in the next billing cycle with prorated adjustments.

What counts as an active user?

An active user is anyone who logs in during the billing period—students, instructors, and admins all count. Inactive accounts don't count toward your limit.

Is there a free trial?

Yes! Start with our Starter plan (free up to 20 users) for 14-day to experience the full platform.

What if we're between 1,000-3,000 users?

We offer flexible Enterprise SaaS plans with volume discounts for institutions in the 1,000-3,000 user range. Contact our sales team for custom pricing tailored to your specific needs—you'll benefit from economies of scale while getting dedicated support.

What if we grow past 3,000 users?

Our Enterprise plan scales beyond 3,000 users with fully custom pricing, multi-region hosting, and dedicated infrastructure. We'll work with you to design a solution that fits your unique requirements and budget.

How is AI usage billed?

With our BYOK model, you connect your own AI provider account (OpenAI, Anthropic, Google AI), giving you complete control and transparency. Our dashboard helps you track usage and optimize costs. Typical AI costs: ₹20-50 $0.25-0.55 per user/month paid directly to your chosen provider.

Is setup and onboarding included?

Yes! All plans include guided onboarding. Pro and higher tiers get dedicated setup assistance and training sessions for your team.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards, ACH transfers, and wire transfers. Enterprise clients can request custom payment terms and invoicing.
