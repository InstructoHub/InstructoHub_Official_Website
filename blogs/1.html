What Is a Learning Management System (LMS) and Why Does It Matter in 2026?

A Learning Management System (LMS) is a platform that delivers, manages, and tracks learning online across schools, universities, and professional training programs.

In 2026, AI-powered LMS platforms are seeing rapid global adoption through digital infrastructure expansion, adaptive learning technology, and the need for scalable, mobile-first education systems.

The real question is no longer "how do we move learning online?"

It is "how do we personalize education at scale and prove it is working?"
